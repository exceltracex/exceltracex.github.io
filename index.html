FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which versions of Excel are supported?
ExcelTraceX is built as a VSTO add-in for Excel on Windows (desktop). It is intended for modern versions of Microsoft 365 / Office Excel on Windows. Excel for Mac and Excel Online are not supported.
How long can I use ExcelTraceX for free?
The free download includes a 1-month trial with full tracing functionality. During this period you can use ExcelTraceX on your own workbooks and decide whether you want to continue with a 1-year Premium license.
What happens after the 1-month trial ends?
After the trial period, ExcelTraceX will require a valid 1-year Premium license key to continue working. You can purchase a key on Gumroad and enter it in the License Manager dialog inside the ExcelTraceX ribbon tab.
Is the installer different for trial and Premium?
No. There is only one installer (the MSI you download from this site). It starts in trial mode, and later you can upgrade the same installation by entering your Premium license key. No need to reinstall.
Where can I get support or report a bug?
The easiest way is to use the contact or support information on the Gumroad product page. You can also reach out using the support link shown inside the ExcelTraceX ribbon in Excel.
